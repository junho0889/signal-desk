# Product Vision

## One-Line Definition
`SignalDesk` is a personal mobile intelligence app that ranks market keywords, themes, and sectors using evidence a single operator can verify quickly.

## Problem
Market attention signals are fragmented across news, search trends, disclosures, and price reaction. The review cost is high, so strong signals are often missed or weak signals consume too much time.

## Goal
Reduce time-to-judgment from discovery to watchlist decision by presenting ranked signals with traceable evidence and clear change context.

## Primary User
- A single self-directed retail investor/operator using the app personally
- Comfortable evaluating catalysts, price context, and risk before acting
- Wants fast triage and evidence-backed review, not automated recommendations

## Primary Review Workflow (MVP)
1. Open Home to scan the top ranked keywords and major movers since last review.
2. Enter Ranking to sort/filter by score change and novelty.
3. Open Keyword Detail to validate evidence (source mix, trend direction, recent events, and linked symbols).
4. Decide one action per keyword: ignore, add to watchlist, or keep monitoring.
5. Receive alert prompts for watchlist items with material score shifts, then re-run the same detail review.

## Product Principles
- Show evidence before interpretation.
- Prefer explainable scoring over opaque claims.
- Optimize for fast mobile review loops.
- Treat alerts as review prompts, never trade instructions.

## Explicit Non-Goals (MVP and Release-1)
- No broker integration, order routing, or auto-trading.
- No social feed, copy trading, or shared/public watchlists.
- No AI chat copilot that generates thesis or trade advice.
- No discretionary portfolio analytics or PnL tracking.
- No desktop/web client requirement for end users.
