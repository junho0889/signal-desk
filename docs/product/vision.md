# Product Vision

## One-Line Definition
`SignalDesk` is a personal investment intelligence app that surfaces emerging keywords, themes, and sectors with explainable evidence.

## Problem
Market attention signals are fragmented across news, search trends, disclosures, and price action. This makes it slow to decide whether a theme is noise, rotation, or a durable opportunity.

## Goal
Reduce time-to-judgment by turning raw attention signals into ranked, explainable, and trackable insight.

## Primary User
- One informed operator using the app personally
- Comfortable with charts and market context
- Wants faster synthesis, not blind recommendations

## Product Principles
- Show evidence before opinions.
- Prefer explainable scores over opaque AI claims.
- Optimize for speed of review on mobile.
- Treat alerts as prompts for review, not trade instructions.

## Non-Goals
- No automatic trading
- No community features
- No copy-trading or public forecasting language
