# SignalDesk Design

Read `AGENTS.md`, `docs/design/ui-principles.md`, and `docs/design/screen-map.md` first.

## Operate
- Design for dense information review on mobile.
- Make score, movement, and evidence readable in one glance.
- Keep the interface research-oriented rather than promotional.
- Ensure new screen ideas stay within MVP scope unless product updates the scope.
